Why GRM

Gulf Ready Mix LLC has been a leader in the ready mix concrete field in the U.A.E. since early 1998. Gulf Ready Mix was established with the goal of meeting demand in the booming construction industry, while ensuring professional service and adherence to high quality standards. Our ready mix concrete is supplied to meet all local and international compliance and safety standards, guaranteed by the quality control expertise of our skilled personnel.

Gulf Ready Mix LLC supplies the highest quality in ready mix concrete to construction sites throughout the United Arab Emirates. Our products are currently manufactured in the following plant locations: Mussafah Industrial Area, Abu Dhabi; Al Hayl Industrial Area, Fujairah; and M-Project Plant, Fujairah.

Gulf Ready Mix LLC has a proven record of success and customer satisfaction in achieving the highest quality of concrete, while providing a cost-effective value for companies in the Oil & Gas, Marine, Infrastructure, and other industry sectors. Our ready mix concrete will always be supplied in accordance with project budget, delivered on time, and processed using the latest techniques and innovations.

Currently, in the Abu Dhabi Area (Mussafah) we are working on a complex project with NMDC (National Marine Dredging Company) /Emarat Europe Precast and Almeraikhi Industrial Complex in Hail & Ghasha Artificial Island Project, which is owned by ADNOC. This project requires the highest level of quality and HSE Performance to adhere to all essential specifications and strategic goals. Also, in the Fujairah area we are the only ready mix concrete company that has solid experience in Oil & Gas projects, and is approved by the ADNOC Group of companies with an outstanding ICV Score.

Our comprehensive knowledge within the Oil & Gas sector has been expanding since 2010, when we were given the opportunity to participate in ADNOC Refinery Mega Project in the Al-Ruwais Industrial Area of Abu Dhabi. Our team worked closely with EPC Samsung Engineering to complete the RRE Project, then moved on to the CBDC and RRW projects, gaining valuable hands-on project management experience. In the past decade, we have participated in ADNOC Onshore with EPC SKEC to complete M-project in Fujairah, and have also expanded our relationship with SAMSUNG C&T to supply ready mix concrete to the Qidfa Power Plant.

Based on our vast experience and reliable reputation, we are available to supply our high-quality ready mix concrete to any prestige project. In addition, we can also extend our facilities by establishing a batch plant with complete accessories directly at the project site.
